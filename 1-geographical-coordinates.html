Geographical coordinates

Using Google Maps we can obtain the geographical coordinates of USC UPC. Here are a few ways:
  • Search for a "USC UPC" and right click the pin
  • Right click on the map in the UPC area and select What's here, then right click the pin.

Encoding Geographical Coordinates

The following are equivalent ways to encode geographical coordinates for USC UPC:
  • Latitude as Northing, longitude as Easting in degrees: 34.02152, -118.28716
  • Latitude as Northing, longitude as Westing in degrees: 34.02152, 118.28716
